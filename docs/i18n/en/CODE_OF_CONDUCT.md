# English (en) — Code of Conduct
> [Language selector](../../LANGUAGES.md) · English source governs.

We commit to an open, professional, harassment-free community regardless of experience, background, identity, ability or viewpoint. Be respectful, specific and constructive; critique ideas, accept correction, help newcomers and protect sensitive information. Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, unwanted sexual attention, spam, deception, irresponsible disclosure and false authorship are unacceptable. Report privately. Maintainers may warn, remove content, or restrict participation, handling reports confidentially and consistently. This applies to issues, PRs, discussions, reviews, releases and project representation.
